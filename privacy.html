1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how The Drawer at Dawn (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the OneMinute Insight mobile application (“Service”). This policy is designed to comply with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) of the Republic of Korea and global standards such as the GDPR.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Items: Email address, Name, Profile image (if provided), User ID.
  • Collection Method: Collected via Supabase Authentication (e.g. Google login) upon registration.

2.2 Subscription & Transaction Information

  • Items: Plan type (Free/Trial/Premium), Start/End dates, Transaction IDs (RevenueCat, App Store, Google Play), Purchase history.
  • Note: We do not store your actual credit card information directly; it is processed by the App Store or Google Play.

2.3 Usage & Technical Data

  • Items: Reading history (opened briefs), Quiz/Mission progress, Saved cards.
  • Technical Data: IP address, Device type, OS version, App version, Access logs, Error traces.
  • Tracking IDs: Advertising IDs (IDFA/GAID) may be collected by third-party tools (e.g. RevenueCat) for attribution, subject to your OS settings.

3. Purpose of Collection and Use

We process your data for the following specific purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Authentication, providing content, managing subscriptions.
  • Personalization: Tailoring news briefs and missions to your usage patterns.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage data to fix bugs and enhance features.
  • Fraud Prevention: Preventing duplicate free trials or abuse.
  • Notifications: Sending service updates or daily reminders (only with your consent).

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract Performance: To provide the Service you requested.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with tax or consumer protection laws.
  • Legitimate Interests: Security, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
  • Consent: For marketing notifications or optional data collection.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes above.

5.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account or withdraw consent.
  • Subscription Records: Retained for 5 years as required by the Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (Korea), if applicable.
  • Server Logs: Retained for up to 3 months for security audits.
  • Trial Prevention Data: A pseudonymized hash is retained for up to one (1) year after account deletion to prevent trial abuse.

6. Destruction Procedures and Methods (PIPA Compliance)

Once the retention period expires or the purpose of collection is achieved, we promptly destroy your personal data.

  • Destruction Procedure: Data flagged for deletion is separated from active storage and permanently deleted after the retention period.
  • Destruction Method: Electronic files are permanently deleted using technical methods that make the data unrecoverable. Printed materials (if any) are shredded.

8. International Data Transfers

To provide the Service, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your residence (e.g., EU Member States, United States). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or reliance on adequacy decisions where applicable (e.g., Korea-EU Adequacy Decision).

9. One-Time Trial Enforcement (Pseudonymized Data)

To enforce the "One-Time Premium Trial" policy, we generate a non-reversible, pseudonymized hash of your account identifier. This hash is used solely to check trial eligibility and is not used to identify you personally. It is retained for up to 1 year.

10. Your Rights

You (and your legal representative if under 14) have the right to:

  • Request access to copies of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., turn off notifications).
  • Object to processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@dawn-drawer.com.

11. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If we discover such data has been collected without parental consent, we will delete such data without undue delay, in accordance with applicable law.

12. Security Measures

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Technical: Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest; access controls.
  • Administrative: Minimizing access to personal data to authorized personnel only.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any material changes at least 7 days in advance through the App or via email.
